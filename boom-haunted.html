About the Game

You play as a dramatic, over-the-top ghost trapped in a tiny haunted room with the world's most clueless NPC — Timmy. Your only goal? Haunt him. Annoy him. Possess him. Make sure he knows he's not alone… even if he desperately wishes he was.

Explore the room, pick objects to possess, and unleash chaotic hauntings just to watch his reactions spiral. Enjoy the pure, stupid, delightful haunting energy.

It's spooky, it's silly, it's unhinged, and every scare comes with a punchline that hits harder than Timmy's mom's roasts.
